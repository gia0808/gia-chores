I took tap dance alongside ballet at Cherry Creek Dance, and I loved the rhythm and energy that came with it. Like ballet, we practiced and performed twice a year at the Newman Center, and the tap numbers were always some of the most upbeat and crowd-pleasing.

Tap challenged my coordination in a new way, and it helped strengthen my timing, balance, and stage presence. It also gave me a deeper appreciation for musicality and the joy of movement through sound.
